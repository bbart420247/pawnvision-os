Why It Travels

The workflow is industry-agnostic.

If the business depends on incoming items, photo evidence, category review, pricing support, and controlled handoff into inventory, the operating pattern is similar even when the merchandise changes.

What Changes

The prompts, comps, and verification rules shift by category.

Luxury, electronics, collectibles, jewelry, and specialty equipment each carry their own evidence needs, pricing logic, and risk profile. The system is meant to adapt to those differences instead of flattening everything into one template.
